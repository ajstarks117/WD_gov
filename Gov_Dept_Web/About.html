About Us

Empowering Indian Agriculture

Working towards sustainable agricultural growth and farmers' welfare

Our Mission

To promote sustainable agriculture through modern technologies and ensure food security while enhancing farmers' welfare across India.

Our Vision

To transform Indian agriculture into a modern, sustainable, and globally competitive sector while ensuring prosperity for farmers.

Key Objectives

  • Increase agricultural productivity through sustainable farming practices and modern technology adoption
  • Ensure food security and safety while promoting agricultural exports
  • Improve farmers' income and welfare through various government schemes and initiatives
  • Promote climate-resilient agricultural practices and natural resource management
  • Strengthen agricultural marketing infrastructure and promote value addition
  • Enhance collaboration between research institutions and farmers for knowledge transfer

Our Rich History

Decades of Agricultural Excellence and Service to the Nation

Independence and Agricultural Reform

Establishment of the Ministry of Agriculture post-independence to address food security challenges and agricultural development in newly independent India.

Green Revolution

Implementation of the Green Revolution, introducing high-yielding varieties of seeds and modern farming methods, transforming India's agricultural landscape.

Introduction of technology-driven initiatives and establishment of agricultural research institutions across the country.

Digital Agriculture

Launch of e-governance initiatives and digital platforms to enhance agricultural services and farmer connectivity.

Sustainable Agriculture

Focus on sustainable farming practices, climate-resilient agriculture, and doubling farmer income through innovative policies and technologies.

About the Department

The Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare is the apex body for formulation and administration of rules, regulations, and laws relating to agriculture in India. We work closely with state governments to implement various schemes and programs aimed at agricultural development and farmers' welfare.


Our department oversees critical areas including crop husbandry, agricultural extension, soil and water conservation, agricultural mechanization, and plant protection. We are committed to achieving sustainable agricultural growth while ensuring food security for the nation.
