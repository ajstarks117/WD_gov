About Us

Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

The Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare is the apex body for formulation and administration of rules and regulations and laws relating to agriculture in India.

Our mission is to promote sustainable agriculture and ensure food security for the nation while improving farmers' welfare through effective policies and programs.
